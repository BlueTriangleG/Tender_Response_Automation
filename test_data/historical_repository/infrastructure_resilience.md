# Infrastructure Resilience and Deployment Notes

## Source

- Client: Public sector shared services tender
- Submission year: 2024
- Domain: Infrastructure

## Approved Positioning

### Availability

The standard enterprise deployment target is 99.9% monthly service availability. Higher availability commitments can be discussed for dedicated environments with agreed scope and cost.

### Business continuity

The platform is designed for multi-zone deployment. The documented recovery point objective is 15 minutes and the recovery time objective is 4 hours for the managed production service.

### Deployment models

The preferred delivery model is managed SaaS. Single-tenant virtual private cloud deployment is available for customers with isolation requirements. Limited customer-managed deployment patterns are possible through a separately scoped professional services engagement and are not part of the default product offer.

### Data location

Regional hosting options are available in Australia, the European Union, the United Kingdom, and the United States, subject to the selected cloud provider and service dependencies.
