# Compliance and Privacy Baseline

## Source

- Client: Regulated services response pack
- Submission year: 2025
- Domain: Compliance

## Approved Positioning

### Certifications and assurance

The organization maintains ISO 27001 certification and SOC 2 Type II reporting for the core platform environment.

### Privacy operations

The service supports data subject access, export, correction, and deletion workflows. Verified right-to-erasure requests are operationally targeted within 30 days, subject to legal retention requirements.

### Residency and transfers

Customer environments can be provisioned in supported Australian regions for clients with local residency requirements. Cross-border data movement is limited by deployment design, subprocessors, and customer configuration choices.

### Security review support

The team can provide recent penetration test summaries, security policy extracts, and architecture responses under NDA during procurement.
